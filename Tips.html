Workout Tips

Sport

All you need is just a little motivation and time to crush these heart-pumping moves.

1. Squat

How to do: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, lower your hips down, and stand back up.

Reps: 15-20

RELATED: Strengthens legs, glutes, and core.

2. Push up

How to do: Keep your hands shoulder-width apart and lower your body until your chest is near the floor.

Reps: 10-15

RELATED: Works chest, shoulders, and arms.

3. Lunges

How to do: Step forward with one leg, lower your hips, then return to standing.

Reps: 10 per leg

RELATED: Improves leg strength and balance.

4. Plank

How to do: Hold a straight body position on your forearms and toes.

Time: 30-60 sec

RELATED: Strengthens the core and stabilizes posture.

5. Jumping jacks

How to do: Jump while spreading your legs and raising your arms, then return.

Time: 30-45 sec

RELATED: Boosts heart rate and improves endurance

6. Triceps Dips (Use a Chair)

How to do: Sit on a chair, grip the edge, lower your body, then push back up.

Reps: 12-15

RELATED: Tones the back of the arms.

7. High knees

How to do: Run in place while bringing your knees up to waist level.

Time: 30-45 sec

RELATED: Cardio and leg strength.

8. Glute Bridges

How to do: Lie on your back, bend knees, lift hips, and squeeze your glutes.

Reps: 15-20

RELATED: Strengthens glutes and lower back.

9. Bicycle Crunches

How to do: Lie down, lift your legs, and twist your torso to touch elbow to opposite knee.

Reps: 15 per side

RELATED: Strengthens the abs and obliques.

10. Wall sit

How to do: Lean against a wall, lower into a squat position, and hold.

Time: 30-60 sec

RELATED: Builds lower body endurance.
